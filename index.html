About Kevin

I graduated from Butler University in May of 2017, and after struggling with the job search, determined that I did not have enough experience to enter the workforce. People have always told me that I would make a good programmer and I have enjoyed learning languages previously, but never believed I would do it as a career. I decided to give Eleven Fifty Academy a try, and have not regretted the decision for a second. On top of strengthening my technical background, I have been forming new relationships, gaining a lot of self-confidence, and really enjoying myself. I cannot wait to see what my future holds.
